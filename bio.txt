Griffin Hennessy grew up skateboarding the sidewalks of Los Gatos and Santa Cruz, California, making amateur skate films with his friends fueled by the dream of becoming a pro skater. After finishing a Business AA, Hennessy moved to Olympia, Washington, where he worked many jobs, painted and made art enamored with the grungy punk rock basements of the PNW. During the pandemic, Hennessy made his way back to Santa Cruz, working at hospitals in support services, setting up vaccination sites until he found a job at a local Screen Printing Shop. His employer encouraged him to continue painting and he decided to return to school to study art at University of Santa Cruz.
